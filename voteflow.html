The first step is to make sure you're registered.
All you need is your National Insurance number and your home address. https://www.gov.uk/register-to-vote

Do a quiz to see which parties policies you most cloesly align to. It's important to remember that parties can change their policies at any time - just because they have said they'll do it in a manifesto it doesn't mean they actually will.

Find our who your candidate are with this open sourced list of all the candidates standing in 2015. ynmp.com

Hustings. Lets face it they're boooring. But if you're going to vote for someone you may as well size them up for yourself in person instead of picking the name that sounds best on the paper. meetyournextmp.com
